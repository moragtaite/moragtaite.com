More about me

I am currently persuing a PhD at NUI Galway under the supervision of Prof. Louise Allcock. I am funded through the Irish Research Council Government of Ireland Postgraduate scheme and Dr. Tony Ryan Research scholarship. I am interested in the evoltion of cephalopods using both molecular and morphological data.
